Beef Rendang is one of my favorites. It is a spicy dish cooked for a long time in a coconut based sauce, traditionally served with rice and sometimes fried anchovies and cucumbers.

Real Rendang takes a long time to make, so I've simplified the recipe by using a spice packet and not cooking it for hours. But someday soon I'll post a lengthier recipe!

Beef Rendang Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of Beef
  • Rendang Spice Packet
  • 1 inch of ginger
  • 1 can of coconut milk

Topping ideas:

  • Diced Cucumbers
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Mandarin Oranges
  • Fried Egg

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet. When oil is sufficiently hot, throw diced beef in. Wait for it to sear, then mix and add salt or any other seasonings desired.
  2. While beef cooks, throw in the spice packet and ginger.
  3. Pour in can of coonut milk
  4. Let the mixture cook on low heat until coconut milk is warmed.
